# CR-011: CAPA-001: Add revision_summary to SOP-005

## 1. Purpose

Correct the nonconformance identified in INV-002 by adding the required `revision_summary` field to SOP-005 frontmatter.

## 3. Rationale

**Nonconformance:**

INV-002 documented that SOP-005 was approved and made EFFECTIVE without the required `revision_summary` field in its frontmatter. This violates:
- SOP-001 Section 5.2 (document frontmatter requirements)
- SOP-002 Section 8 (CR traceability requirements)

**Corrective Action:**

Add the missing `revision_summary` field referencing CR-010, which was the authorizing CR for SOP-005's initial creation.
