# Document Is Stuck

Common blocking reasons:

**Can't route for approval:**
A reviewer submitted `request-updates`. Address their feedback, [check in](../edit/checkin.md), and [re-route for review](../workflow/route-review.md).

**Can't close:**
Child documents (VARs, TPs, VRs) must reach required states first. [Check status](../find/status.md) of all children.

**Document is locked:**
Another user has it checked out. [Check who has it](../find/status.md).

**Not sure what state it's in:**
[Check document status](../find/status.md).
