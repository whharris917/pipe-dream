# Document Types

### Executable Documents

Contain an executable block with execution items (EIs). Go through pre-approval, execution, and post-approval phases.

**Types:** CR, INV, TP, ER, VAR, ADD, VR

### Non-Executable Documents

Define requirements or procedures. Follow a simpler DRAFT > REVIEW > APPROVAL > EFFECTIVE lifecycle.

**Types:** SOP, RS, RTM

See `QMS-Docs/02-Document-Control.md` for full details.
