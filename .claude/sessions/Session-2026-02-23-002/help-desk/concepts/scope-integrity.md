# Scope Integrity

**Approved scope cannot be silently dropped.**

Every approved EI must have a documented outcome:

- **Pass:** Task completed as described, with evidence.
- **Fail:** Task could not be completed. Requires a child document ([VAR](../create/child/var.md) or [ER](../create/child/er.md)) explaining why.

You cannot delete, skip, or mark an EI as "N/A" without a VAR.

See `QMS-Docs/06-Execution.md`.
